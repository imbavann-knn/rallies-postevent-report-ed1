Section 02

Event Overview & Brand Story

Pickleball is the fastest-growing sport in Southeast Asia. ACUVUE RALLIES tapped into that momentum to create a first-of-its-kind competitive league.

The Rallies Concept

Rallies is a community-first competitive pickleball platform that brings together grassroots "Kampong Kaki" players and corporate teams. Edition 01 featured 278 players across 36 teams competing in group stages at Singapore EXPO before Grand Finals at the iconic Plaza Singapura Level 1 Atrium.

By staging finals in a mall atrium, ACUVUE RALLIES reached beyond the sport's core audience — converting casual shoppers into spectators and brand engagers. Peak concurrent spectator count exceeded 350 during the Grand Finals.

Event Atmosphere — Atrium Crowd Shot

Why It Matters Now

Singapore's pickleball community has grown exponentially. ACUVUE RALLIES positions the brand at the intersection of wellness, active lifestyle, and community — reaching a highly desirable working-age demographic (84.9% aged 25-54).
